Data definitions & methodology
Internal reference — PeopleConnect SEO / Martech
Data sources
SnowflakeSales / gross adds. Supermetrics refresh weekly. Source: bi_layer_db.prod.exp_daily_sales_metrics
Google Search ConsoleImpressions, clicks, CTR, avg position. Data begins Nov 24, 2025. Source: PC_FIVETRAN_DB.GSC
BigQuery / GA4Bucket test sessions and purchases. Refreshes daily. Source: ga-bigquery-319917.analytics_472677824
ProfoundAEO (Answer Engine Optimization) metrics — Visibility Score, Share of Voice, Average Position, Citation Share. Source: tryprofound.com
GSC — Google Search Console
Total ClicksMeasures the number of users who clicked from a Google search result to your website.
Total ImpressionsTracks how many times your website appeared in search results, acting as a leading indicator of visibility.
Average CTRThe ratio of clicks to impressions. A higher CTR indicates your title tags and meta descriptions are compelling.
Average PositionIndicates where your pages rank on average in the SERPs. A lower number is better.
AEO — Answer Engine Optimization
Visibility ScoreThe relative position of your brand compared to other brands based on how often it appeared in AI-generated answers.
Share of VoiceYour brand's ranking based on how frequently it is mentioned across prompts versus other brands in your space.
Average PositionYour brand's ranking compared to other brands based on its average position in AI-generated answers. Note: the rank is only calculated among the top 50 most visible brands.
Citation RankYour brand's position compared to others based on how often your website is cited in AI-generated answers.
Metric definitions
Gross addsNet new paid subscriptions attributed to SEO / Organic channel for the period.
% to goalGross Adds / Target. Values above 100% indicate goal was exceeded.
Organic conv rateGross Adds / GSC Clicks. Exception: OmniWatch uses GA4 Organic Sessions as denominator.
Avg positionAverage Google Search ranking for brand pages. Lower is better.
Weekly periodFriday–Thursday. Anchored to 2026-01-01 (a Thursday) via modular arithmetic.
OmniWatch is excluded from core brands totals. It is measured on Organic channel (Direct + Organic Search + Organic Social), not SEO-only, and its conversion rate is not comparable to other brands. GSC data prior to Nov 24, 2025 is unavailable. AEO data sourced from Profound; USSearch has no AEO presence in the current period.
